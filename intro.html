Welcome to Your Meditation Tracker

Embark on a journey of mindfulness and self-discovery with our Meditation Tracker. This tool is designed to support and motivate you in establishing a consistent meditation practice.

How to Use Your Meditation Tracker

1. Set Up Your Practice

  • Choose a name for your meditation journey
  • Select your challenge duration: 48 days or 90 days
  • Pick your start date
  • Click "Start Tracker" to begin

2. Track Your Daily Sessions

  • Find two buttons for each day: "M" (morning) and "E" (evening)
  • Click a button to mark a session as complete
  • Watch the button turn green to celebrate your achievement
  • Click the notebook icon to add notes about your session
  • Look for an orange dot on buttons with notes

3. Monitor Your Progress

  • Check your dashboard for an overview of your journey
  • View your current streak and total meditation time
  • See how many days are left in your challenge
  • Explore the weekly overview for a visual representation of your progress

4. Dive Into Your Statistics

  • Access detailed stats about your practice
  • Learn your completion rate and longest streak
  • Discover your average daily meditation time

5. Enjoy Guided Meditations

  • Find a new meditation prompt each day
  • Listen to the provided audio for guidance
  • Use these as inspiration for your practice

6. Share Your Journey

  • Use the "Share Progress" button to celebrate your achievements
  • Inspire others with your dedication to mindfulness

7. Customize Your Experience

  • Toggle between light and dark themes for comfortable viewing
  • Export your data to keep a record of your progress

Tips for a Successful Practice

  • Aim for consistency: Try to meditate at the same time each day
  • Start small: Even a few minutes daily can make a big difference
  • Use the notes feature: Record your insights and experiences
  • Celebrate milestones: Take pride in your progress, big and small
  • Be kind to yourself: Remember, it's called a practice for a reason

Remember, the journey of meditation is unique for everyone. This tracker is here to support you, but the real growth happens in the moments of stillness you create each day. Embrace the process, be patient with yourself, and enjoy the path to greater mindfulness.

Happy meditating!
